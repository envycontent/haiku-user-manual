Google Analytics - Add an additional Account¶

This shows you how to add an additional account to an existing Google Analytics account. If you want to set up a new Google Analytics account see Google Analytics - Set up an Account.

Help on Google Analytics is available from Google at: http://support.google.com/analytics/

Admin section¶

Login to your Google Anaytics account and click on the Admin link on the top right hand side of the page.

Account adminstration¶

Click on the drop down list under the Account heading.

Sign up for a Google Analytics account¶

Click on Create new account

Account details¶

  1. Make sure that Website is selected.

2. Select Classic Analytics. Then scroll down the page and enter details of your site:

Details¶

  1. Enter an account name (for example the name of your department).
  2. Enter the name of your website.
  3. Enter the web address of your website (select http:// from the drop down box on the left).
  4. Select your industry.
  5. Select your timezone.

6. Decide on your Data Sharing Settings. You can get more information on this from the Google website by clicking on the question mark icon next to the Data Sharing Settings heading. 7.Click on Get Tracking ID.

Accept terms and conditions¶

  1. Check that United Kingdom is selected.
  2. Click on the I Accept button.

Your tracking code¶

Once you have accepted the terms and conditions you will be taken to a page with your tracking code. Email us (turnkey@medsci.ox.ac.uk) this tracking code so that it can be included on all your webpages.
