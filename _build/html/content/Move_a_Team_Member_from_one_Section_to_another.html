Move a Team Member from one Section to another¶

You can move team members from one column to another in the Our team section using the cut and paste functions in the Contents display. This shows you how.

Our team - Contents display¶

Go to the Our team section of the website and click on Contents.

Our team - sections¶

Click on the section where the person you would like to move is.

Select the person you would like to move¶

  1. Click in the check box next to the name of the person you would like to move.
  2. Click on Cut.

Go back to the team sections¶

Scroll up to the top of the page and click on the ^ Up one level button to return to the Our Team section names.

Our team - sections¶

Click on the section you would like to move the person to.

Move the profile¶

Click on paste.
