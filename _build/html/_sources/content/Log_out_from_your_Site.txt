
Log out from your Site
======================================================================================================

How to Log out from your website. 	

Find the Log out link
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Log_out_from_your_Site/media_1349168742576.png
   :align: center
   

Click on your name in the top right hand corner of the screen to view the following drop down list:


Log out link
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Log_out_from_your_Site/media_1349168861208.png
   :align: center
   

Click on Log out


Log out of from your Single Sign-On account
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Log_out_from_your_Site/media_1349169026672.png
   :align: center
   

Click on Logout to log out from your Single Sign-On user account.


Complete the Single Sign-On user account log out process
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Log_out_from_your_Site/media_1349169217363.png
   :align: center
   

Click on Proceed with Single Sign-On Logout and close your browser to complete the Single Sign-On log out.
