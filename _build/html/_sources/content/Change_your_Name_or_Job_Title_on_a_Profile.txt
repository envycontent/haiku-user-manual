
Change your Name or Job Title on a Profile
======================================================================================================



Open your profile
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Change_your_Name_or_Job_Title_on_a_Profile/media_1401803035970.png
   :align: center
   

Go to your profile and click on **Edit** on the right of the page.


Personal information edit box
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Change_your_Name_or_Job_Title_on_a_Profile/media_1401803066237.png
   :align: center
   

Bring your cursor over your name to bring up the **Edit** button. Click on the **Edit** button.


Edit box
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Change_your_Name_or_Job_Title_on_a_Profile/media_1401803155590.png
   :align: center
   

Make the required changes and click on the **Save** button in the bottom right hand corner.


Save your profile
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

.. image:: images/Change_your_Name_or_Job_Title_on_a_Profile/media_1401803205471.png
   :align: center
   

Click on the **Save layout** button to save your profile.
